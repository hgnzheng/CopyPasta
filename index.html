Korean Surgical Patient Data Provide Insights for Medical Research and Clinical Practice

VitalDB has released a dataset of 6,388 surgical patients from a Korean hospital, providing high-frequency physiological waveform data, demographics, clinical records, and post-operative outcomes. This resource supports medical research, AI-driven diagnostics, and precision medicine. Users can filter data by department and gender to analyze common surgical conditions. The dataset is available in compressed CSV format for easy access and analysis. This release aims to enhance surgical risk assessment, optimize perioperative care, and improve treatment predictions, driving innovation in surgery and healthcare worldwide.
