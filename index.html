Korean Surgical Patient Data Provide Insights for Medical Research and Clinical Practice

This webpage provides an interactive tool for analyzing the most common diseases among surgical patients in a Korean hospital. Users can filter data by selecting specific departments and patient sex to view the top five diseases for each category. The dataset encompasses information from 5,345 surgeries, including high-frequency biosensor data and corresponding medical records.
